ABOUT

Asra was born in Berlin but grew up in Iceland. When 20 years of age she moved to Tokyo, Japan where she studied Japanese Literature and Art History at Waseda University. She is currently working as a graphic designer with a Japanese company in Tokyo.
Her independent work is writing, which initially led her on her quest into the world of Japanese literature. As a writer she aspires to explore and reevaluate human behaviour, our relationship with the earth, and its various vast surroundings.
